Main Feature Story

Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK) is a Tamil Nadu political party founded by actor Vijay on February 2, 2024, with its headquarters in Panaiyur, Chennai. The party, led by "Thalapathy" Vijay, aims to contest upcoming elections and has chosen a whistle as its official election symbol. It focuses on fighting corruption and divisive politics, with a reported 15 million+ members as of September 2025.
